Cliff to Sky

Description

Cliff to Sky is a local, small press whose focus is on independent authors and aspiring writers. They are like the cozy, local coffeehouse where the baristas know you by name. They needed a cohesive system to express their desire to work with the local community of authors to inspire their readers to engage with their content. The communication approach would be friendly, personable, and down-to-earth to contrast that with the big publishing houses’ corporate focus. The feather represents the author’s journey when they publish their first book. The type choice is to reflect the down-to-earth vibe of the brand.
