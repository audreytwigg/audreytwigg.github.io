Unyielding

Description

Unyielding is a visual novel and RPG game about a mercenary named Catelly who is robbed of her identity. She must go through perilous adventures to seek revenge and discover who she is. There are many game mechanics such as character customization, upgrades, libraries, and a combat-mode. The challenge was to create an interface for a story-heavy game with large amounts of narrative (over 200k words), while simultaneously highlighting the artwork. Alongside designing the interface and creating the assets, I created several digital artworks that are used as backgrounds. The game is developed and written by MercernaryMage and the character art is by 5ish.
