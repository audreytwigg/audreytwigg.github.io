Rebel Mystic

Description

Rebel Mystic is an introductory kit on divination that explores the magical and the occult for those beginning their journey into the mystical. The kit includes a 78-card tarot deck, 24 Elder Futhark runes, 2 instructional guides, and 2 notebooks for each topic. My goal was to provide a welcoming introduction to divination for those who are new to that world. Rebel Mystic is for curious rebels who are bewitched with the world of divination.
