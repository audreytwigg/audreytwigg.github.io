Hide

Description

Hide is an interactive graphic novel about a girl who is bullied at home and school. She must decide whether to succumb to the dark emotions or triumph over them. I aim to explore user experience design principles to create an immersive result. By delving into dark topics such as mental health, abuse, bullying, and gender issues, I speak directly to my audience through the narrative. As an aspiring developer and designer, I am passionate about crafting a compelling product dedicated to the female gaming audience who craves thoughtfully provoking storytelling.
