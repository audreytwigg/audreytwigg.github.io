PROJECT
SHED THE LIGHT
DESCRIPTION

Shed the Light is a browser-based tower defense game with a low-poly geometric style being developed by Tato. The objective was to create cohesive UI that enhances the game’s fantasy and narrative while organizing the large amount of content that is typical with a strategy defense game. I had to balance the information that’s shown on the screen at once, during gameplay, and during build mode. The client had existing assets that I matched to complete the entire interface.
